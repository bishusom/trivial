Unleash Your Inner Jedi: The Ultimate Star Wars Trivia Challenge (50 Questions & Answers)

May the Force be with you as you test your knowledge of a galaxy far, far away! This ultimate Star Wars trivia challenge features 50 questions, ranging from Padawan-level easy to Jedi Master-level hard. Get ready to jump to lightspeed and see how well you know the Skywalker saga and beyond!

Easy Star Wars Trivia Questions

  1. What is Luke Skywalker's home planet?

Answer: Tatooine

  1. Who is Luke Skywalker's mentor in A New Hope?

Answer: Obi-Wan Kenobi

  1. What famous line does Darth Vader say to Luke Skywalker in The Empire Strikes Back?

Answer: "No, I am your father."

  1. What type of weapon does a Jedi use?

Answer: Lightsaber

  1. What is the name of Han Solo's ship?

Answer: The Millennium Falcon

  1. What species is Chewbacca?

Answer: Wookiee

  1. What droid famously says "Beep boop beep"?

Answer: R2-D2

  1. What is the name of the evil galactic empire in the original trilogy?

Answer: The Galactic Empire

  1. Who is Princess Leia's adoptive father?

Answer: Senator Bail Organa

  1. What is the Force?

Answer: An energy field created by all living things that binds the galaxy together.

  1. What color is Darth Vader's lightsaber?

Answer: Red

  1. What is the name of the desert dwelling people on Tatooine?

Answer: Tusken Raiders (or Sand People)

  1. What pet does Luke Skywalker have on his moisture farm?

Answer: A power converter (jokingly, he doesn't have a traditional pet)

  1. What iconic phrase is said before a jump to hyperspace?

Answer: "Punch it!" or similar variations.

  1. What is the name of the cantina in Mos Eisley?

Answer: Chalmun's Cantina

  1. Who shot first in the cantina scene between Han Solo and Greedo?

Answer: Han Solo (according to George Lucas)

  1. What type of vehicle does Luke Skywalker pilot on Tatooine?

Answer: A landspeeder

  1. What is the name of the forest moon where the Ewoks live?

Answer: Endor

  1. What is the Rebel Alliance's main base in The Empire Strikes Back?

Answer: Hoth

  1. What is the rank of Grand Moff Tarkin?

Answer: Grand Moff

  1. What is the name of the parasitic creatures that control the Geonosians?
