Masterpiece Challenge! Test Your Art & Architecture Knowledge: Top 50 Trivia!

Easy Level

  1. Who painted the Mona Lisa?

    Answer: Leonardo da Vinci

  2. What iconic Parisian structure was built for the 1889 World's Fair?

    Answer: The Eiffel Tower

  3. Which artist cut off part of his ear?

    Answer: Vincent van Gogh

  4. What ancient wonder was located in Alexandria, Egypt?

    Answer: The Lighthouse of Alexandria

  5. Who designed the Guggenheim Museum in New York?

    Answer: Frank Lloyd Wright

  6. What is the main material used in the construction of the Taj Mahal?

    Answer: White marble

  7. Which painting features a scene of farmers in a starry night?

    Answer: The Starry Night by Van Gogh

  8. What Greek structure is dedicated to the goddess Athena?

    Answer: The Parthenon

  9. Who sculpted David in the Renaissance era?

    Answer: Michelangelo

  10. What architectural style features pointed arches and flying buttresses?

    Answer: Gothic

  11. Which artist co-founded Cubism with Georges Braque?

    Answer: Pablo Picasso

  12. What is the name of the leaning bell tower in Pisa, Italy?

    Answer: The Leaning Tower of Pisa

  13. Who painted the ceiling of the Sistine Chapel?

    Answer: Michelangelo

  14. What is the largest art museum in the world?

    Answer: The Louvre (Paris)

  15. Which architect designed the Sydney Opera House?

    Answer: Jørn Utzon

Medium Level

  1. What art movement is associated with Salvador Dalí and melting clocks?

    Answer: Surrealism

  2. Which American architect designed Fallingwater?

    Answer: Frank Lloyd Wright

  3. What ancient Roman temple became a church to save it from destruction?

    Answer: The Pantheon

  4. Who painted The Birth of Venus?

    Answer: Sandro Botticelli

  5. What is the name of the iconic glass pyramid at the Louvre entrance?

    Answer: The Louvre Pyramid (designed by I.M. Pei)

  6. Which Dutch artist painted Girl with a Pearl Earring?

    Answer: Johannes Vermeer

  7. What architectural style is characterized by simplicity and functionality?

    Answer: Modernism

  8. Who designed the spiral ramp for New York’s Guggenheim Museum?

    Answer: Frank Lloyd Wright

  9. What art movement was led by Claude Monet and Pierre-Auguste Renoir?

    Answer: Impressionism

  10. Which building is considered the tallest in the world as of 2023?

    Answer: Burj Khalifa (Dubai)

  11. What is the full name of the artist who created The Persistence of Memory?

    Answer: Salvador Domingo Felipe Jacinto Dalí

  12. Which architect pioneered the use of reinforced concrete?

    Answer: Le Corbusier

  13. What Japanese art form involves folding paper?

    Answer: Origami

  14. Who designed the iconic "Glass House" in Connecticut?

    Answer: Philip Johnson

  15. Which artist is known for The Scream?

    Answer: Edvard Munch

  16. What German art school operated from 1919 to 1933?

    Answer: Bauhaus

  17. What structure in India is often called a monument to love?

    Answer: The Taj Mahal

  18. Which sculptor created The Thinker?

    Answer: Auguste Rodin

  19. What is the name of the famous unfinished portrait by Leonardo da Vinci?

    Answer: Saint Jerome in the Wilderness

  20. Who painted the American Gothic farmhouse scene?

    Answer: Grant Wood

Hard Level

  1. What is the original name of the Statue of Liberty?

    Answer: Liberty Enlightening the World

  2. Which architect designed the Barcelona Pavilion?

    Answer: Ludwig Mies van der Rohe

  3. What Renaissance artist wrote a famous notebook mirrored in reverse?

    Answer: Leonardo da Vinci

  4. What is the oldest surviving monumental structure in Egypt?

    Answer: The Step Pyramid of Djoser

  5. Who designed the Vietnam Veterans Memorial in Washington, D.C.?

    Answer: Maya Lin

  6. Which artist’s final work was the Rondanini Pietà?

    Answer: Michelangelo

  7. What is the term for the triangular upper part of a Greek temple?

    Answer: Pediment

  8. Who painted the Arnolfini Portrait?

    Answer: Jan van Eyck

  9. What architectural feature did Filippo Brunelleschi design for Florence Cathedral?

    Answer: The dome

  10. Which artist founded the Art Nouveau movement in Barcelona?

    Answer: Antoni Gaudí

  11. How many years did it take to build the Taj Mahal?

    Answer: Approximately 22 years

  12. What is the name of the only surviving Wonder of the Ancient World?

    Answer: The Great Pyramid of Giza

  13. Who designed the iconic Chrysler Building in New York?

    Answer: William Van Alen

  14. Which painting technique uses wax as a binder for pigments?

    Answer: Encaustic

  15. What is the term for the vertical groove in a Greek column?

    Answer: Fluting
