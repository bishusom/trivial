Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2025

1. Information We Collect

When you play Triviaah, we may collect:

  • Gameplay data (scores, categories played, performance metrics)
  • Device information (browser type, operating system)
  • High score information including player names (if provided)
  • Analytics data via Google Analytics

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and improve our trivia game service
  • Maintain leaderboards and high scores
  • Analyze gameplay trends and improve question quality
  • Understand user engagement through Google Analytics

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored:

  • High scores are stored in Firebase Database
  • Gameplay analytics are processed by Google Analytics
  • Some data may be cached locally on your device

4. Third-Party Services

We use:

  • Google Analytics - To understand how users interact with our game
  • Firebase - For storing high scores and player data

These services have their own privacy policies which we recommend reviewing.

5. Your Choices

You can:

  • Opt not to provide a player name (scores will be recorded anonymously)
  • Clear local storage data using your browser settings
  • Use browser extensions to block analytics tracking

6. Children's Privacy

Triviaah is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. The "Last Updated" date at the top will reflect changes.

8. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions, please contact us using the Contact Us Form
